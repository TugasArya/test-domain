About Me

Hi, I'm Aura 👋

I am a multidisciplinary digital artist and independent music producer exploring the intersections of dreamscapes, internal emotion, and nostalgic sonic fields. My visual work fuses surrealist portraiture with lo-fi aesthetics, while my sound relies heavily on textural synthesizers and organic rhythms.

Whether crafting sound waves or brush strokes, my objective is always the same: building digital environments that feel deeply human, comforting, and personal.
